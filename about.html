Kyungeun is currently a Senior Lead Data Scientist at NBCUniversal. As a member of a decision science team, she helps executives make informed decisions using data. Before transitioning to industry, her research focused on better understanding our universe through the searches for dark matter and neutrinos. She received her PhD in Physics at Columbia, completed postdoctoral research at Yale. Besides having fun with data science, she likes to go to museums, to collect eye witness travel guides of the places she has been, and to work out especially Zumba. She is also a big fan of Haruki Murakami, Astor Piazzolla, Gustav Klimt, and Elsa Schiaparelli.
